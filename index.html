Neon Blade is an action and exploration 2D game with cyberpunk ambience. You will impersonate Haro and, helped by a sword, a stun gun and a hook you shall defeat foes room by room, buy upgrades, and challenge the floor boss to get to the next level. So...what are you waiting for?

What Happened

About Us

    From the memes and the flames came an awful team with the mission of create an awesome game. They fought against terrible foes disguised as compiler errors and static_cast, but they manage to overcome all obstacles in their way. And at the end of their journey, they finally complete this cursed quest, giving birth Neon Blade. Now, this team only hope it please their evil teachers, and also you, dear reader.
